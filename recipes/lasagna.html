Lasagna

"A stacked layer of pasta, minced beef, and bechamel sauce"

Ingredients

  • Pasta
    • 9 lasagna noodles
    • 1 tsp salt
    • 5 tbsp oil
  • Meat Filling
    • 1 pound ground beef
    • 5 cloves of garlic
    • 1 white onion
    • 500 grams italian style tomato sauce
    • 1 tsp italian seasoning
    • salt and pepper to taste
  • Bechamel Sauce
    • 1/2 cup butter
    • 1/2 cup flour
    • 1/2 can evaporated milk
    • 1 tsp nutmeg
    • salt and pepper to taste
  • Toppings
    • mixed cheddar and mozarella cheese

Steps

  1. Boil water in a pan in high heat. Once it bubbles, turn it to medium heat and add the lasagna sheets. Put some oil and salt to it.
  2. In another pan, saute the garlic and onion. Once it turn medium brown, add the ground beef and cook it 'till its oil comes out.Then, add the tomato sauce and season it with salt, pepper, and italian seasoning.
  3. For the bechamel sauce, melt butter until slightly brown. Add flour, make sure to combine well. Then, slowly add the milk to avoid lumps. Lastly, add nutmeg, salt, and pepper.
  4. In a platter, first add the meat filling. Then, layer it with the lasagna sheets. Spread the meat filling topping it with bechamel sauce. Repeat layering twice more, finishing with mixed cheddar and mozarella cheese.
  5. Bake in preheated oven until cheese melts and sauce bubbles, about 90 minutes. Cover lasagna with foil if cheese begins to get too dark. Let sit 10 to 15 minutes before serving.
